WHO ARE WE?

Welcome to Serpabox, the boxing gym where everyone has a place! Here, the most important thing is not your level, age or gender, but your passion to improve. Whether you want to compete professionally or you are simply looking for a place to do sport, take care of your health and strengthen your discipline, we are ready to help you get the best out of you.

Under the guidance of Sergio, with his vast experience, and surrounded by a close-knit community where everyone supports each other, you will discover the true spirit of this noble sport. At Serpabox, you will fall in love with boxing as you reach your goals, step by step, join our family and live the experience!
